Microsoft|Pro Tip of the Day
Exam Insight
⚡
Tip #1 of 30
Start with
OneLake
Shortcuts
Before learning pipelines, understand how OneLake Shortcuts let you access external data without copying it. The exam frequently tests when to use Shortcuts vs Pipelines — Shortcuts are often the right choice.
🔗
What is a Shortcut?
A virtual link to data in ADLS Gen2, Amazon S3, or Google Cloud Storage that appears as a folder inside your Lakehouse — no data movement.
🎯
Exam Focus
Know the difference between native tables, OneLake shortcuts, and Query Acceleration shortcuts in Real-Time Intelligence.
